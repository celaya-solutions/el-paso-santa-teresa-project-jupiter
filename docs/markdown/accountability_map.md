#### **New Mexico Governor's Office**
**Action**: Signed State MOU with BorderPlex Digital Assets LLC on 2025-02-25  
**Citation**: State MOU dated 2025-02-25, signed by Governor  

**Legal Exposure**:
1. **Misrepresentation of Environmental Law** - Stated natural gas qualifies as "zero carbon resource" (State MOU lines 115-125), contradicting HB93 requirements and scientific fact
2. **Premature Commitment** - Committed state support 7 months before local approval process, potentially pressuring county decision
3. **Public Funds Commitment** - Committed to subsidize brackish water drilling costs (State MOU lines 16-22) without legislative appropriation
4. **Regulatory Fast-Tracking** - Committed to expedited permitting (State MOU lines 37-43), potentially circumventing environmental review requirements

**Statutory Violations Alleged**:

- Appropriations process (commitment of funds without legislature)

**Enforcement Mechanisms**:
- Writ of Mandamus to enforce compliance with HB93
- Legislative oversight hearings
- Attorney General investigation of ultra vires acts
- Taxpayer lawsuit for unauthorized commitments
