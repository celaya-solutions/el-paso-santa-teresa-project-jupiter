## **KEY ACCOUNTABILITY NOTES**

**Who voted YES (4-1 vote on Sept 19, 2025):**
- Schaljo-Hernandez, Gameros, Reynolds, Sanchez

**Who voted NO:**
- Susana Chaparro (only one)

**Legal Issues:**
- DOJ complaint filed by resident Derrick Pacheco alleging Open Meetings Act violations
- NM Foundation for Open Government also alleged violations
- Concern: Commissioners went into closed session without proper notice during Sept 19 vote

**Transparency Problems:**
- Final agreements NOT released before vote
- County staff still hadn't released updated documents weeks after vote
- Reporter had to get docs from Project Jupiter directly, not county
- Most negotiations happened under NDAs

**Oversight Mechanisms (according to county):**
- County taking over water/wastewater from CRRUA in 2026 = direct oversight
- EPA, NM Environment Dept, Public Regulatory Commission permits still required
- County can pursue civil action for "public nuisance"
- **But:** No explicit enforceable numeric limits on water use in agreements
